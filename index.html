About

Hi there! I'm Peter and I am an aspiring web developer with a passion for front-end development, JavaScript, CSS, and React! I graduated from Marquette University in December of 2014 with a degree in Marketing and Economics. I've worked in the advertising field as an account manager since my college graduation, but am very excited to move into the field of web development.

In June 2017 I began teaching myself how to code. Since that day I've been absolutely enthralled with everything relating to web development.

As a young developer I align my priorities with industry standards and modern trends. I enjoy coding websites using semantic HTML as well as employing accessibility standards in order to reach the broadest audience possible. I also prefer component-based architecture using libraries and frameworks like React and SASS in order to write maintainble, quality code.

In my free time you'll find me reading, hanging with friends and family, watching and playing soccer, or enjoying a new TV show or movie.
